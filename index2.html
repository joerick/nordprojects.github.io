Hi we’re Nord Projects.

We’re a design and technology consultancy. We work like a startup to build products that delight customers and solve real business problems.

We love making things.

We’ve been working together since 2012 on projects like Tingbot and Mixim. These are the projects we love best - where great design and solid engineering make the complex simple.

We work across mobile, web and connected devices. We like to work on:

Strategy

Design

Development
Build technology and provide a codebase that's an asset to your business.
